# SharpEdge — Investment & Partnership Overview

---

## Executive Summary

**SharpEdge** is a Discord-first sports betting intelligence platform that democratizes access to professional-grade betting analytics. By combining real-time odds aggregation, AI-powered research, and institutional-quality tools, SharpEdge enables recreational bettors to bet smarter and track their performance like professionals.

**Market Timing:** Legal sports betting is now live in 35+ US states with $100B+ wagered annually. The market for betting tools and analytics is nascent but growing rapidly.

**Business Model:** SaaS subscription via Discord and web/mobile ($19.99–$49.99/month Pro/Sharp), leveraging Whop for payments and access control.

---

## The Problem

### For Recreational Bettors

1. **Information Asymmetry**: Professionals have tools worth $10,000+/year. Recreational bettors have nothing.

2. **No Edge Identification**: Bettors don't know when they have an edge or are being exploited.

3. **Poor Bet Tracking**: Most bettors don't track performance or understand why they win/lose.

4. **Time-Intensive Research**: Comparing lines, tracking movements, and finding value takes hours daily.

5. **Emotional Betting**: Without data, bettors rely on gut feelings and lose long-term.

### Market Gap

| Segment | Tools Available | Cost | Accessibility |
|---------|----------------|------|---------------|
| Professional Bettors | CRIS, Pinnacle, custom models | $5,000-50,000/year | Exclusive |
| Sports Media | ESPN, Action Network | Free-$100/year | High |
| Recreational Bettors | **Nothing purpose-built** | N/A | Gap |

**SharpEdge fills this gap** — professional-grade tools at recreational-friendly prices.
