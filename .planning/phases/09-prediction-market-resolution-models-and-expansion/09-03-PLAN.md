<!-- Locked feature vector from CONTEXT.md decisions. -->

Universal features (all 5 categories, always present):
  market_prob     = mid of yes_bid + yes_ask / 2  (Kalshi) or outcome.price  (Polymarket)
  bid_ask_spread  = yes_ask - yes_bid  (Kalshi) or 0.0  (Polymarket — single price)
  last_price      = last_price  (Kalshi) or outcome.price  (Polymarket)
  volume          = volume
  open_interest   = open_interest  (Kalshi) or liquidity  (Polymarket proxy)
  days_to_close   = (close_time - now).days  (Kalshi) or (end_date - now).days  (Polymarket)

Category add-ons (appended after universal 6):
  political:  [polling_average: float, election_proximity_days: int]
  economic:   [days_since_last_release: int, is_release_imminent: int (0/1)]
  crypto:     [underlying_asset_price: float, price_change_7d: float]
  entertainment: []  (market-only)
  weather:    []  (market-only)

Category detection — event_ticker prefixes (Kalshi) and question keywords (Polymarket):
  political:   ticker starts with "KXPOL" OR question contains "election|president|senate|congress|vote|ballot|poll"
  economic:    ticker starts with "KXFED"|"KXCPI"|"KXGDP"|"KXNFP" OR question contains "inflation|cpi|fed rate|gdp|unemployment|nonfarm"
  crypto:      ticker starts with "KXBTC"|"KXETH"|"KXSOL" OR question contains "bitcoin|btc|ethereum|eth|crypto|solana|coinbase"
  entertainment: ticker starts with "KXENT"|"KXOSC"|"KXGRM" OR question contains "oscar|grammy|emmy|award|box office|film|celebrity"
  weather:     ticker starts with "KXWTH"|"KXHUR" OR question contains "hurricane|temperature|rainfall|flood|snow|tornado"
  default:     "entertainment" (market-only features, safest fallback)
